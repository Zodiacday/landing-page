Privacy Policy

Donezo Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Donezo ("the Extension") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Data We Collect

Currently (Free Version)
  • Task data & preferences (such as your to-do list, dark mode setting, and progress).
  • Stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.
  • Synced to your Google account only if you have Chrome Sync enabled.
  • No personal data (such as your name, email, or payment information) is collected at this time.
Future (Premium Features)
  • Email address (to create and manage accounts).
  • Payment information (processed securely through a third-party provider such as Stripe or Google Payments, never stored by us).
  • If these features launch, this policy will be updated before collection begins.

2. How We Use Data

  • To store and sync your tasks and preferences.
  • To provide core extension functionality.
  • [Future] To manage premium subscriptions and deliver paid features.

We do not:

  • Sell or rent your data.
  • Use your data for advertising.
  • Share your data with third parties except as required for payments (Stripe/Google Payments) or by law.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All free version data is stored locally in Chrome storage.
  • Premium data (when available) will be stored securely in encrypted databases.
  • We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no system is 100% secure.

4. Your Choices

  • You can delete your tasks and preferences anytime within the extension.
  • You can uninstall Donezo to remove all locally stored data.
  • [Future] You may request deletion of your account and subscription data.

5. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as Donezo evolves. If significant changes are made, we will notify users through the extension or our website.
